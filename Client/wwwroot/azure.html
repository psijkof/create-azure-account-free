How to create a free Azure Account

Create an account on Azure and explore the free services to practice or use for personal purposes

Prerequisits

Credit Card

In order to create an account on Azure, you need to have a valid credit card. You could create a card with your bank app. In Portugal some people use MBWay, which allows you to make credit cards for shopping for example. You could make a card with a limited spending amount.

Microsoft Personal Account

You will need a Microsoft Account (Personal or School/Work). You can create one here, if you donâ€™t already have one.

Azure Portal

Go to portal.azure.com

Sign in with your (newly created) Microsoft Account. You will be signed in without subscription.

Create a subscription

Currently Azure offers a trial of 30 days, where you can spend $200 for free.

No automatic charges. After your credit is finished and or your trial is over, you will be asked if you want to continue with pay-as-you-go. If you do, you will only pay if you use more than the free amounts of services.
